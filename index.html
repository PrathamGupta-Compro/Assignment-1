CHAPTER 1

Misselthwaite Manor

ary Lennox was ten years old and she lived in India with her parents. Her father was always busy with his work and her mother was always busy with parties and friends. They didn’t have time for Mary, so an Indian nanny looked after her.

The nanny allowed Mary to do anything she wanted because she didn’t want Mary to cry. So Mary became a spoilt, unpleasant girl with a bad temper. She was often ill and had a thin, sad face.

One hot morning Mary woke up and heard strange cries and shouts outside her door. But no one came to see her, so she went back to sleep.
